Welcome to DeliverMe, a platform to move product on a personal scale.


DeliverMe is designed to help you in your everyday life. Think Uber, but for items instead of people. Restaurant orders, grocery orders, furniture from Ikea that is too big for your car... any object that you need help transporting and do not have the time or space to pick up yourself. DeliverMe has you covered. To use the app, simply decide if you want to request a delivery by choosing "New Job" or if you want to deliver someone else's items for them by choosing "View Jobs" to see the list of current items waiting to be picked up. With this user-friendly app, your life will become easier in no time!
